Whether it is for business trips, family fun, or competition; Capt John Lee has you covered. Big Time Charters is a locally owned charting service, that offers both offshore and inshore trips. For a bit of an understanding of the two, offshore is situated at sea some distance from the shore and inshore is still at sea, but closer to the shore.

Inshore

Interested in shark fishing. We have you covered. Want to do some trolling, while fishing for grouper? Can do!

Offshore

We offer dive trips to many of our artificial reefs and wreckages, and while we're out, enjoy a beautiful sunset cruise.

Fishing trips ranging from 4, 6 & 8 hours; will be fishing for Kings, Spanish Mackerel, Blues, Little Tuna, Blue Dolphin, and Cobia. Fishing trips ranging from 8, 10 & 12 hours; will be fishing for Grouper and Red Snappers. For other inquires, you can contact us. Fishing licenses, tackle and equipment will be included in the trip. Just bring food, drinks and yourself. Be prepared for a great time!

Capt John Lee is no stranger to these beautiful beaches. He was born in Panama City, FL and has lived in Mexico Beach, FL for the last 36 years. For the last 20 years, he's been a Charter Captain. He captains a 36'x 14' Sports Fishermen sporting a 7 kW diesel generator, the latest fishing electronics, air conditioning and heating. He has his USCG 100 Ton Master License and is a certified diver, who is always up for a dive!
