Poisoning is injury or death due to swallowing, inhaling, touching or injecting various drugs, chemicals, venoms or gases. If you're concerned about possible poisoning, call Poison Help at 800-222-1222 or your regional poison control center. Be ready to describe the person's symptoms, age, weight, and any information you have about the poison. Try to determine the amount exposure and how long since the person was exposed to it. If possible, provide the name of the of the suspected chemical when speaking to the poison control center.

If a person appears unconscious, has difficulty breaking or has stopped breathing, having seizures or appears to have been exposed to large amounts of chemicals immediately dial 911. If a fire is out of control immediately dial 911.
